only the front shell files have changed from v2.0 to v2.1
 
- improvements to the button wells:
	- widened to prevent cracking
	- chamfered at the top to improve screwing
	- filleted at the bottom to improve durability

its a good idea to use mineral oil to lube the screw posts while tapping the threads for the first time to reduce friction and get a crisper thread. this should minimize the chance of resin crack even further and make tapping a *breeze*
